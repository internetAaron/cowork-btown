Buckle Down B-Town
Feb 1st, 10am

What's Buckle Down? It's a workday to help local businesses.

  1. Find three local businesses that need help.
  2. Recruit great designers, devs, project managers, marketers, biz-dev, and strategists.
  3. Design and Development talent form teams.
  4. Each team chooses a leader from the marketing or project management talent.
  5. Match team with a business according to its needs.
