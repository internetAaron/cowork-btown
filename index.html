About us

Welcome to the workplace of the future! Cowork Btown offers affordable workspace and exciting social events for professionals of all sorts – right in the heart of downtown Bloomington. Although the space encourages collaboration, Cowork Btown is not just for entrepreneurs. The organization attempts to leverage the flexibility of the new working world, gathering likeminded small businesses, artists, musicians, and remote workers that love (and hope to keep their talents in) Bloomington.

Current coworkers range between 22 and 43 years old and include Bloomington natives, one-time residents of other Midwestern states, and migrants from both coasts. All coworkers take their work very seriously and are expected to respect the time and space of others, but their office hours vary drastically.

Cowork Btown is currently located at 117 West 6th Street, just across from Pheonix Comics. There are a limited number of dedicated desks still available and abundant “drop-in” workspaces.

Membership Plans

Designated Desk

Includes permanent desk and chair, the freedom to do with them as you please, and 24/7 key access.

 $255/month

Drop-In

We can't guarantee a desk to yourself, but there's always somewhere to sit! Drop-ins are invited to hangout at the space up to 15 hours each week, may attend all after hours events, and are able to rent the conference room twice per month. Sorry, no key, but doors are open from 8:00 AM to 7:00 PM most days.

 $65/month

Day-to-Day

Here, everyone's welcome. While events and space rental are reserved for our members, we encourage all community members to stop in and see what we're about. If you feel like hanging out, please consider leaving a small donation – a coffee's worth is more than enough.

Where is Cowork Btown?

We are located in the beautiful town of Bloomington, IN. We just moved into our space not even a block off the square! Our space has a large open working area with several dedicated desk and drop in work stations. We have two meeting rooms. One is more traditional with a conference table and whiteboard. The other is more casual with comfortable seating and a TV. If you'd like to visit us feel free to drop by during normal work hours through the week or keep an eye out for one of our open events.
